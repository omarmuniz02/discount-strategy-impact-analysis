# Discount Strategy Impact Analysis

SQL and Tableau analysis evaluating how discount strategies affect revenue, gross profit, and margin performance.

## Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Overview](dashboard_overview.png)

## Business Problem

A VP of Sales noticed that revenue was growing, but profitability was not improving as expected. The company had been offering increasingly aggressive discounts to drive sales, raising concerns that discounting might be eroding margins rather than creating sustainable growth.

## Project Objective

Analyze how High (>20%), Medium (10–20%), and Low (<10%) discount tiers affect revenue, gross profit, margin percentage, and units sold across product categories, sales channels, and regions during 2023–2024.
